Take Them to School

"Take Them to School" is an impactful initiative dedicated to children's education.

Our mission is to break barriers for children facing adversity by providing them with educaitonal opportunities through sponsorship programs. By nurturing their potential and granting access to education, we aspire to pave the way for brighter futures. Join us in shaping tomorrow's leaders and fostering inclusive learning environments for all children, regardless of their backgrounds. Together we can make a difference, one child at a time.

It all started 5 years ago with two young girls and at the present moment, we are proud to announce that our initiative has successfully sponsored over 30 children ranging from 5 to 17 years old, in either primary or secondary schools. Our approach ensures that these children have access to public schools and boarding schools, depending on their needs. We equip them with all the essential supplies needed to thrive academically, including uniforms, school bags, notebooks, and school lunches.

Furthermore, our collaboration extends beyond the classroom, involving school teachers, generous donors, dedicated sponsors, and the supportive communities surrounding the children's families. It's through this collective effort that we can extend our reach and impact. As we continuously gather support, we envision expanding our reach to empower even more children to pursue their dreams through education. Join us in transforming lives, one sponsorship at a time.
