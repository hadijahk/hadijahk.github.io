About Us

Golden Heart is a non-registered NGO operating in Dar es Salaam that primarily focuses on children coming from challenging backgrounds. We provide them with support, education, and the opportunity to cultivate a sense of belonging and community.

Our vision is to create a community formed under the principles of peace and love towards vulnerable children.

Our mission is to promote the development of the most vulnerable children in Tanzania through the creation of working forums that empower children.

Current Projects

Take Them to School

Our mission is to break barriers for children facing adversity by providing them with educational opportunities through sponsorship programs to pave the way for brighter futures. We are proud to announce that our initiative has successfully sponsored over 30 children to attend school ranging from 7 to 17 years old.

Learn More

Sport (Talent Development)

This project is currently situated in Kunduchi – our primary objective is to nurture the students' sport talents, offering a safe and engaging environment that steers them away from the streets. Currently there are 14 children in our program that benefit from the sports equipment we provide to them.

Learn More

Orphanage Care

This project focuses on visiting different orphanages, which is important because we want to connect with the children. By doing so we can bring them day to day necessities, teach them new things, and play games with them.

Learn More

Awareness

This project is dedicated to spreading awareness within the communities, schools, and wherever there is a need. We tailor our efforts based on the current circumstances and challenges faced by the communities.

Upcoming Projects

Golden Angels School

The envisioned establishment will be a nursery school – the inspiration for this initiative stems from the challenges faced by children in Madale, a village with existing nursery schools often inacessible during rainy days due to the surrounding river that floods frequently. To avoid dangerous river crossing, Golden Angels School will help children access education close to home.

Learn More

Hold My Hand

This initiative seeks to offer children who are not in contact with their parents a sense of family and belonging. In Tanzania adoption is exceptionally challenging. To still be able to support children to the fullest, in the Hold My Hand project we aim to meet every aspect of the child's needs. Despite not physically residing with you these children will come to recognize and appreciate you as a parental figure in their lives.

Learn More

Rafiki

This project aims to forge a meaningful connection between Tanzanian schools, orphanages, children, and institutions from foreign countries. By fostering these international connections, we aim to provide Tanzanian children with the opportunity to interact with peers from around the world, thereby expanding their horizons and facilitating cross-cultural exchange.

Learn More

Child Sponsorship

This project gives people the opportunity to sponsor a child, ensuring their every need is met, extending beyond solely educational requirements. The sponsorship provides comprehensive assistance to improve the lives of these children, including providing access to education, healthcare, and nutritous meals.

Learn More

Ndoto Yangu

This project focuses on empowering girls with teen pregnancies to continue their education to pursue their dreams. While education is a cornerstone, we recognize that independence and self-suffiency extend beyond the classroom – we strive to provide opportunities that foster long-term resilience and autonomy.

Learn More

Golden Adventure

This program aims to bring children a sense of community through picnics, school trips, summer camps, and safaris. These experiences are tailored to promote teamwork, curiosity, and joy, helping children develop a deeper appreciation for nature, culture, and one another.
